Aug 15, 2020

So yesteday I was looking around data.world for some ideas; which later lead me to The U.S. Census Bureau page.

That is where I stumbled upon data for e-commerce. They have data from 1999 to present about how much of our commerce is e-commerce. And it got me wondering at what point will the percent level out?

So sometimes the best way to see data is to visualize it. So let us graph it and see if we can get some insights.

We get a trendline (not shown) with the formula:

y=0.0013x2 + 0.342x + 1.5047

and a R2 value = 0.9958

This clearly indicates on-line purchases as a percent of total purchases are growing. With our current Covid-19 situation; this is likely to climb even higher in the following quarters as numbers get reported to the US Census Bureau.
