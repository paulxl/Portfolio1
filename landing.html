Garnet Coding

This is a coding portfolio page. Periodically I will be expanding the content and apps.

It is not really about Garnets; though there is a link above leading to some information and pictures.

I was a born rock hound and have a BS in Geology and think Garnets are cool.

As for the red Zia logo: It on a yellow background is New Mexico's state flag/logo. A few years ago while I was living there, the state put the Zia with a similiar blue background on some of their licence plates and it really grabbed me. I also earned my degrees from New Mexico State University. The exposed geology and a chance to see history in the rocks is facinating. While doing some field work, I had the opportunity to come across rocks dated back over a billion years old that are now exposed. New Mexico is an amazing land with a wide variety of climates and landscapes. My family settled in the area in 1880's, so growing up I got to hear a lot of great history.
